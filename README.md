PRIVACY POLICY
Tarot Card Reading Interpretation

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tarot Card Reading Interpretation ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.


1. OVERVIEW

Tarot Card Reading Interpretation is designed to work primarily offline. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not sell your personal information.

The App provides tarot card readings using a built-in Rider-Waite deck and stores certain information locally on your device to improve your experience.


2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on our servers. The App does not have user registration or cloud sync.

Information stored locally on your device may include:

- Reading history (spread type, cards drawn, date and time of reading)
- Optional intention or question text you enter before a reading
- Favorite cards you mark in the library
- Your daily card selection
- App preferences (such as reversed card settings)
- Whether you have completed the introductory onboarding screens

This information stays on your device unless you uninstall the App or clear the App's data through your device settings.


3. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect:

- Name, email address, phone number, or account credentials
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
- Payment or billing information
- Advertising identifiers for tracking purposes
- Analytics or usage data sent to us

The App does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or other sensitive device permissions for its core features.


4. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

Information stored on your device is used only to:

- Display your past readings in the History section
- Remember your favorite cards
- Show your daily card for the current day
- Save your app settings and preferences
- Provide the features you choose to use within the App

We do not use this information for advertising, profiling, or marketing.


5. DATA SHARING

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties.

Because data is stored locally on your device, we do not transmit your reading history or intentions to any server operated by us.

Google may process technical information such as your IP address when fonts are requested. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy at:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will assist with removing locally stored data by advising you to uninstall the App or clear its data.


8. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

All App data is stored on your device. You can delete this data at any time by:

- Clearing the App's storage/data in your device Settings, or
- Uninstalling the App from your device

We do not retain copies of your data on our servers because we do not receive your data.


9. SECURITY

We take reasonable steps to design the App so your information remains on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure.


10. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or delete data. Because the App stores data only on your device, you can manage or delete that data directly through your device settings without contacting us.


11. INTERNATIONAL USERS

The App is intended for use worldwide. Any information you enter is stored locally on your device in your country of use. We do not transfer your data internationally because we do not collect it on our servers.
